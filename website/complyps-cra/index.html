What is the EU Cyber Resilience Act?

The CRA (Regulation (EU) 2024/2847) sets mandatory cybersecurity requirements for almost any product with digital elements placed on the EU market — hardware or software that connects to a device or network. Non-compliance can mean fines and market withdrawal.

10 Dec 2024

Entered into force

The regulation is law; the compliance clock starts ticking.

11 Sep 2026

Reporting obligations

Actively exploited vulnerabilities & severe incidents must be reported (24h / 72h).

11 Dec 2027

Main obligations apply

Full requirements, conformity assessment and CE marking become mandatory.
